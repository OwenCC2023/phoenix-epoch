Happiness System:

Main Goal: Describe quantitatively how happy the population is with the government given the population's ideology vs the government's implementation.

General Principles:
	- 0-100 scale. Base is 50.
	- Happiness is generated by trait-policy option pairs.
	- Deviance from desired policy option reduces benefits.
	- Like Stability, Happiness is generated and tracked at the province level.

System Effects:
	- Acts as a multiplier on Worker Productivity with a range of 0.8 to 1.2.
	- Acts as a multiplier on Corruption Resistance with a range of 1.3 to 0.7. Low happiness increases corruption.
	- Acts as a multiplier on Growth/turn (provincial) with a range of 0.9 to 1.1.
	- Acts as a multiplier on Stability Recovery/turn (provincial) with a range of 0.9 to 1.1.
