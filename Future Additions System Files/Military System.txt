Military System

Main Goal: Simulate the ability of a nation to build and maintain a military of diverse unit types, capabilities, and quality levels.

- Military DP Categories:
		- Three DP categories per branch: Strategy, Tactics, and Logistics.
		- Strategy DPs unlock more Doctrines within the Doctrine System (new stub).
		- Tactics DPs unlock more sophisticated tactics within the Combat System (stubbed).
		- Logistics DPs unlock the capacity for more Formations and Groups.
		- Logistics DP unlock greater supply statuses for Units.
